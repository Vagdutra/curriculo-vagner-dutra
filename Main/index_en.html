About Me

I work in the tech industry with a focus on systems support, data analysis, and critical operations assistance. My expertise lies primarily in ERP environments, troubleshooting, business rules validation, and technical analysis aimed at operational stability.

I currently work as a Systems Analyst at FUNCORSAN, supporting core systems and analyzing financial, pension, and operational processes using Oracle Database, SQL Developer, and PL/SQL.

With a degree in Business Administration and studies in IT Management at UNISINOS, this combination enables me to understand both the technical and operational impacts of implemented solutions.

Alongside my corporate roles, I develop personal projects involving process automation, web applications, and management support tools, turning real-world needs into functional solutions.

Beyond technology, I am a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt and instructor. The discipline and ability to maintain clarity in high-pressure scenarios are fundamental traits in my professional life.

I seek opportunities where technology, processes, and business vision must walk hand in hand.
